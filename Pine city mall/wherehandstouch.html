WHERE HANDS TOUCH

Where Hands Touch is a 2018 British-Belgian-Canadian-Irish romantic war drama film directed and written by Amma Asante and starring Amandla Stenberg, George MacKay, Abbie Cornish, Christopher Eccleston, and Tom Sweet. It was produced by Charlie Hanson, and tells the story of Leyna (Stenberg), a biracial girl under threat because of her race, due to her living in Nazi Germany.
