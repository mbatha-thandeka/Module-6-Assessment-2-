PEDROS

Pedros is a flame grilled Portuguese chicken franchise, particularly well-known in Kwazulu-Natal. Since hearing of their Long Street branch opening recently, I decided to pop in and see what the hype is all about. As Long Street is a prime location, off street parking may be a problem during peak hours.
